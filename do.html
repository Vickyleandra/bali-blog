Experience Bali

Find out what Bali has to offer!

Penglipuran

The Penglipuran Village Festival happens every December in order to improve the tourism. It includes a clothing parade and art parades.

Tirta Empul

This temple has waters said to be sacred and it's possible to swim in the pools.

Nusa Lembongan

You will be able to see the reefs and snorkle over the beautiful sealife in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Penida.

Ubud jungle

Marvel at the dense vegetation of the rainforest and don't miss the opportunity to visit the swing.

Pasar Badung

Shopping at Pasar Badung will ensure you take a bit of unique Bali back home.

Bali Zoo

One of the main experiences of the Bali Zoo is to interact with elephants. You'll learn about their lives and care, as well as conservation.

Festivals in Bali

Bali is known for its several parades and religious festivals happening throughout the year. Experience the culture and tradition!

Tabanan

Explore the rice paddies and learn how this grain is grown and harvested.

Temples

Don't miss out on the 20,000+ temples you can visit while in Bali.

Mount Batur

Climbing Mount Batur and watching the sunrise is a great way to connect to nature.

Yoga

Find a yoga barn to enlighten your mind and relax while in a retreat, where you can also detox from stress and technology.
